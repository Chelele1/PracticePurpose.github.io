How to use the test
For wedges, sand, pitches, chips and trouble shots, enter the distance from the target for each of five attempts. A point is earned when the result is at or inside the stated “Leave it at” distance. For short putting, enter the number holed out of four balls. For lag putting, enter the point value earned on each attempt according to the original test setup. The app applies the scoring brackets from the workbook and stores data only on this device unless you export it.
